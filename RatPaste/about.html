What is Rat Paste


Rat Paste is a band from Washington State that incorporates various elements from genres such as Folk Punk, Nu-Metal, and Emo.

The band consists of Bennett on Vocals, Rowan on Drums, Arthur on Guitar, And Mr. Pug is the Bassist, Backup vocalist, Banjo player, and Producer.
